My Philosophy of Care

My journey in medicine has been guided by a simple principle: to blend the highest standards of medical science with genuine, compassionate care. Training at the Post Graduate Institute of Medical Education and Research (PGIMER) in Chandigarh provided me with a rigorous foundation in evidence-based nephrology.

However, I believe that true healing extends beyond prescriptions and lab reports. It begins with listening. It's about understanding your concerns, your lifestyle, and your hopes. My goal is to be your partner in health, demystifying complex conditions and creating a personalized care plan that works for you.

My Mission

My mission is to bring world-class nephrology care to my home community. I am dedicated to creating a resource where patients feel heard, respected, and empowered.

Through NephroFoundation, I aim to provide accessible, reliable, and easy-to-understand medical information to help you navigate every stage of your kidney health journey with confidence.
